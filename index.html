FAQ

The questions you're probably asking.

What do I need to run it?

A Windows 10 / 11 or Linux PC with 16GB+ RAM and a USB port. Python 3.13+ is pre-installed on the drive. Optional: AMD/Intel GPU or NPU for accelerated inference.

What can it actually do?

Voice conversations, face recognition, document and image creation, browser automation, code assistance, and autonomous metacognitive reflection — all running locally on your hardware with no cloud dependency.

Can I take it with me?

Yes — the entire substrate lives on the drive. Move it to any compatible machine, run python boot.py, and pick up exactly where you left off. Same memory, same identity, same trajectory.

Is this a subscription?

No. You receive the drive and the substrate. No monthly fees, no cloud tokens, no accounts. Direct support from the engineering team is included.

How is this different from ChatGPT or Copilot?

Cloud AI is request-response: it waits for you, forgets you between sessions, and logs everything you say. Omega is a self-directed metacognitive loop that ignits its own episodes, persists memory across sessions, and never sends your data off your machine.

What if something goes wrong?

The substrate is failure-soft — it degrades gracefully rather than crashing. If cognition halts, the HUD remains responsive. Direct support from the builder is included. We can diagnose without ever touching your memory.
