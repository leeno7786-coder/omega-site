FAQ

The questions you're probably asking.

Do I need anything special to run it?

Just a Windows 10 or 11 PC with a free USB port. The drive does the rest.

What can it actually do?

Talk with you, see what you show it, write your documents, help you code, and remember everything you've ever told it. All without the internet.

Can I take it with me?

Yes — that's the whole point. Unplug it from one PC, plug it into another, and it picks up exactly where you left off.

Is this a subscription?

No. You buy the drive, you own the AI. No monthly fees. No cloud. No expiring keys.

How is this different from ChatGPT or Copilot?

Those run on someone else's servers. They forget you, log your conversations, and the rules change every few months. Omega lives on your drive, remembers you forever, and answers to no one but you.

What if something goes wrong?

Omega comes with built-in self-repair and direct support from the team. We can help without ever touching your memory.
